KöR Whitening — After Your In-Office Visit
Post-Operative Instructions

Your whitening appointment is complete! Over the next few days, oxygen from the KöR Whitening gel will continue to brighten your enamel. Follow these steps to keep your smile comfortable and achieve your final color.

Right after your visit:

  1. Drink plenty of cool water for the rest of today.
  2. Brush gently this evening with a soft toothbrush and Sensodyne ProNamel® toothpaste.
  3. Avoid very hot or cold foods for the next 24 hours.
  4. Refrain from smoking or using colored mouth rinses until tomorrow.

Tonight and tomorrow:

  1. Follow any home tray instructions provided by our team.
  2. Store whitening gels in the refrigerator when not in use.
  3. Apply KöR Desensitizer as directed.

Over the next week:

  • Continue brushing with a low-abrasive toothpaste morning and night.
  • Rinse with water after coffee, tea, red wine, or soda to maintain brightness.
  • If you feel a short burst of "zing" in a tooth, it's simply oxygen activity within enamel and fades quickly.
  • Keep hydrated and maintain your regular brushing and flossing.

Long-term care:

  • Whitening results are very stable when you follow your KöR Maintenance Plan.
  • We recommend storing trays safely for future touch-ups as needed.
  • Avoid leaving whitening gels at room temperature for extended periods.

Thank you for trusting Colorado Advanced Dentistry with your smile. Enjoy your refreshed, naturally brighter results!
