Thank you for choosing KöR Whitening! While most patients experience little to no discomfort, some may experience sensitivity during treatment. Below are steps you can take to reduce sensitivity and continue your whitening process comfortably.

1. Proper Desensitizer Application

  • Ensure you are applying KöR Desensitizer correctly.
  • Watch the patient video for instructions and follow the technique precisely. Keeping your teeth dry is essential to the product working.

2. Use Sensodyne® ProNamel® Toothpaste

  • Start brushing with Sensodyne ProNamel at least one to two weeks before whitening if you suspect sensitivity.
  • If you develop sensitivity, switch to Sensodyne ProNamel immediately.

3. Take NSAIDs (if safe for you)

  • Take 400-600 mg ibuprofen (e.g., Advil®) during the day and naproxen sodium (e.g., Aleve®) at night, provided you have no medical restrictions.

If you have any concerns or need further guidance, please contact our office. We are here to help you achieve a brighter smile comfortably!
