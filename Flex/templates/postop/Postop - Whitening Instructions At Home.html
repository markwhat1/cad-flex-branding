KöR Whitening — At-Home Instructions
Post-Operative Instructions

Congratulations on starting your whitening treatment! The KöR Whitening System works gently while you sleep to rejuvenate your enamel and reveal your natural brightness. Please follow these simple steps for best results.

When you get home:

  1. Keep whitening gels refrigerated when not in use.
  2. Before inserting trays, brush with a soft toothbrush and non-abrasive toothpaste such as Sensodyne® ProNamel®.
  3. Dry your teeth lightly with tissue or gauze before inserting trays.
  4. Apply a thin line of gel in each tray tooth pocket as directed.
  5. Seat trays firmly. Remove any excess gel with a tissue or cotton swab.

Each morning:

  1. Remove trays and rinse your mouth with cool water.
  2. Brush with Sensodyne ProNamel® toothpaste and rinse your trays under cool water.
  3. Dry trays completely before storing them.
  4. Apply KöR Desensitizer as directed.

Daily comfort and care:

  • Stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • If you notice a temporary "fresh" or "tingling" feeling, that's normal and brief. Continue your routine.
  • Avoid very hot or cold drinks for the next 24 hours.
  • Do not use whitening trays more often than directed by our office.

Diet tips:

  • You may enjoy normal foods and beverages.
  • Rinsing with water after coffee, tea, or wine helps maintain brightness.
  • Avoid highly acidic foods right after removing trays.

Maintenance:

  • Store all whitening products in your refrigerator.
  • Follow your custom maintenance plan for touch-ups as needed.

Questions or concerns?

If you notice irritation or persistent discomfort, pause whitening and contact us at (303) 986-9337 or office@cadentistry.net.
