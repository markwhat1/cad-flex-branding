Pinhole Surgical Technique™ (PST)
Post-Operative Instructions

Instructions for First 6 Weeks

DO these things to help sites heal and keep your mouth fresh
  1. Gently ice on top of cheeks as much as possible for first 48 hours - 10 minutes on, then 10 minutes off & repeat as much as possible.
  2. Brush the tongue gently.
  3. Brush any teeth that did not have surgery on them.
  4. May very slowly & gently brush the lingual (inside next to tongue) of the teeth that had surgery on them.
  5. May use provided mouth rinse to "drool rinse". Turn head side to side and let rinse come out. No gargling, no swooshing, and no spitting.
  6. Eat a healthy diet using softer, easily chewable foods. May blend any healthy foods for a smoothie. See diet instructions on the next page.
  7. Lower sugar intake to maximize healing.
  8. WEAR THE PRESCRIBED BITE APPLIANCE AS DIRECTED. Ask the doctor about any other appliances such as orthodontic retainers.
  9. Return to this office for all post-operative/polishing visits.
  10. After the first 6 weeks of healing, you may brush the surgical area with a special extra soft toothbrush provided by this office for 6 months after surgery.
DO NOT do these things during the first 6 weeks after surgery
  1. Don't do anything that will disturb the surgical sites. Be your own best wound caretaker. Be as gentle as possible for 6 weeks.
  2. No brushing, flossing or Waterpik of the surgical sites.
  3. Do not brush the biting surface of the teeth that had surgery.
  4. No touching the surgical sites with fingers, tongue, or any other object like cotton swabs, picks, etc.
  5. Do not look at the surgical sites. Keep cheek movements to a minimum.
  6. Do not sleep with your hands under your cheek.
  7. No spitting, use of straws, blowing balloons, snorkeling, or use of wind instruments.
  8. NO SMOKING, NO VAPING, no sucking on candies or mints.
  9. Medical use of marijuana should be limited to edibles only.
  10. No heavy aerobics, vigorous dancing, heavy lifting, or physical activity for 1 week.
  11. No scalding hot liquids or alcohol for 1 week after surgery.
  12. No crunchy or sticky foods.

Diet Instructions

Soft Diet Suggestions

  • Daily vitamins!
  • Anything put through a food blender
  • Mashed banana, mashed avocado, or applesauce
  • Mashed or baked potatoes
  • Any mashed/blended fruit unless it has seeds.
  • Broth or creamed soup
  • Mashed steamed vegetables
  • Cottage cheese, cream cheese, or soft cheese
  • Mashed yams, baked sweet potato or butternut squash
  • Eggs any style, with or without melted cheese
  • Omelets can have cheese and avocado
  • Jell-O, pudding, ice cream or yogurt
  • Milk shake/smoothies – fruit is fine if it has no seeds
  • Ensure, Slim Fast – nutritional drinks

Things to Avoid

  • Chewing gum
  • Candy, cookies, chips, nuts
  • Anything hard or crunchy
  • Anything with seeds or hard pieces
  • No meat that shreds that can lodge under the gum or between teeth
  • No raw vegetables or salad
  • NO OATMEAL
