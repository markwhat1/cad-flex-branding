Immediate Dentures
Informed Consent

I understand that I have chosen to do an immediate denture. I have also been given the option to do a traditional denture. I understand that by choosing an immediate denture, this creates the following risks:

  • I understand that for an ideal fitting denture, all teeth should be removed first, followed by at least 8 weeks of healing and impressions began after 8 weeks. The general timeframe from surgery to denture delivery with a traditional denture is 12-16 weeks (I would be without teeth for this entire time). By choosing to do an immediate denture, I understand that I am skipping the healing phase and try-in appointments and by doing so I am choosing a denture that is expected to be ill-fitting and may even be nonfunctional.
  • I understand my denture(s) will be delivered the same day my teeth are being removed.
  • I cannot see the color, shape or size of teeth that I have chosen, in my mouth.
  • I am not guaranteed that my bite, overbite, underbite or overjet will be correct.
  • I understand that the overall esthetics, fit and function of the denture may be compromised.
  • I understand that I will need denture adjustments due to sore spots or other reasons. Included in the price of the denture is 3 denture adjustments and 1 soft reline (a soft inner coating that is placed at the dental office) within the first 6 months of receiving the denture. Any adjustments or relines over the number allotted or completed after 6 months will be at an additional cost.
  • I understand that additional surgery may be needed. Bone spicules or excessive bone is sometimes common after multiple extractions. In this situation, additional surgery such as alveoloplasty or removal of bone spicules may be needed. There may be an additional charge for these procedures.

In most cases, the immediate denture will need to be relined after 6 months of healing and in some cases a completely new denture is necessary. At that time, if I choose to have the denture relined, or if I choose to have a new denture made, these costs will be my responsibility.
